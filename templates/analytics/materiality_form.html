Overall Materiality

The maximum amount by which the financial statements could be misstated without affecting users' decisions.

Performance Materiality

Set lower than overall materiality to reduce the risk that the total of uncorrected errors exceeds overall materiality. Usually 60-75%.

Clearly Trivial

Errors below this threshold are clearly trivial and do not need to be accumulated or reported. Usually 3-5% of overall.
